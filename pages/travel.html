Unsolicited tongue-in-cheek travel advice from a small-town Idahoan living in small-town Germany

It will sometimes (perhaps often) appear that I am complaining about my travels. Although I have on occasion run into some unfortunate, and even painful, circumstances during my times abroad, I have yet to take a trip that I did not thorougly enjoy. I have never regretted any of them.
